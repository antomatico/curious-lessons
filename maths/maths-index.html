Maths isn't just calculations. It's the study of patterns, structures, and truths that hold regardless of the universe they're in. Every topic in this course reveals something surprising — a number that goes on forever, a problem that seems obvious but isn't, or a discovery that changed history.


Start with Lesson 1. Go at your own pace. When the quiz feels too easy, dig into the challenge sections. And when something confuses you — that's the most interesting moment. That's where real maths lives.
